Medicaid Enrollees in New York City Far Surpass Those In Other New York State Social Service Districts, 1997-2013
By Kelly Caputo

New York City Health and Hospitals ended the fiscal year with their lowest cash balance in 15 years, a fact that can be contributed to the amount of Medicaid enrollees in New York, as per my budget story. I compiled a spreadsheet list of the Medicaid enrolles for each of the New York State Social Service Districts from 1997-2013, using data provided by the New York State Department of Health. Using Census data, I then determined the New York State Social Service Districts with the highest populations, and chose the top ones. I created a timeline ineractive bar graph to illustrate the increase in Medicaid enrollees over time, and the stark contrast between those in New York City, in comparison to other districts.
